EGG COLLECTOR

The game has three levels: easy, medium, and hard. To win the game, simply collect all the eggs and avoid the collision of the moving enemy.


Press the arrow left on the keyboard to move to the left.

Press the arrow right on the keyboard to move to the right.

Press the arrow up on the keyboard to move upward.

Press the arrow down on the keyboard to move downward.
